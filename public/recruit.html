Why Recruit?


Maharaja Agrasen College, a constituent college of the University of Delhi, was founded in 1994. In 2005, Training and Placement Cell (TPC) was established.In the present world competition is at its peak and creating a place for self in a professional field is a tough task. While the college provides the students with education and all the necessary skills to excel, the Training and Placement Committee of Maharaja Agrasen College does its best to give them the opportunities to flourish through a common platform, which is shared by both the students and the corporates. The primary motive of TPC is to provide students with employment opportunities.
The Primary Objective of Training and Placement Cell is to bridge gap between the students and industries. We provides placements and internship opportunities to students. Apart from that, TPC also seeks to impart effective communication skills, management skills and all the necessary etiquettes which are of utmost importance. It becomes paramount to sharpen the skills of the students before they graduate because they would be stepping into a whole new world of competition. In order to fulfill the above mentioned goals, TPC keeps organizing interactive sessions for students including seminars, workshops and training sessions.
Students gain insight into how things operate in a professional setting through training and internships. It assist students in becoming familiar with how the technology they learnt are used in the real world. When students enter the workspace as working professionals, this exposure enables them to perform at their highest level. TPC makes it easier for students to receive internships and placements from reputable national and international industries. Many of our alumni are employed by reputable worldwide corporations, and each year, we are setting a new placement record.
Over ten sessions were held online throughout epidemic, twenty organisations offered placement opportunities, and more than eighty students received internships. At the job fair for the differently abled, more than 80 students volunteered. The cell hosted numerous workshops on topics such as content writing, linkedin, and CV making. By conducting workshops, It teaches the students management skills, communication skills, PR abilities, and more. In 2022, we held a number of workshops at the institution, including ones on career counselling, entrepreneurship, interviewing techniques, and FDP on Python.

The Process


Welcome to the structured world of placements. Allow us to walk you through the systematic steps:

  • Pre Placement Talk: Informative session may be conduct to provide an overview of the organization, its operations, and assist students in gaining insights into their expectations.
  • Submission of Data and CVs: Streamline the process by by Providing companny with your data along with your CV.
  • Online Test: For select opportunities, be prepared for group discussions or online assessments. This is your opportunity to showcase your abilities.
  • Interview: Interviews can be either Online, On-campus or at the organization's premises, depending on the convenience of the recruiter.
  • Job Offer: Successful candidates will receive formal job offers. Subsequently, you will liaise directly with the company for additional details.

Guidance for Students:

  • Respect application deadlines; late submissions will not be entertained.
  • The students are advised to check their email and WhatsApp groups (more frequently during the placement week) ,for all Placement Cell related updates.

Your quest for professional success commences here. Let us embark on this journey together!
